Club Sandwich


Prep Time: 30 mins | Cook Time: 45 mins | Servings: 4

★★★★☆

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast 250g
  • 0.5 tsp Peppercorn
  • 4 Eggs
  • 0.4 tsp Vegetable Oil
  • Sliced Bread (12Slice)
  • 4 tbsp Butter
  • 4pcs Lettuce
  • 2pcs Cherry Tomato

Instructions

  1. Season chicken breast with ½ sachet of MAGGI® Magic Sarap®, pepper and paprika. Set aside.
  2. Beat eggs and season with remaining ½ sachet of MAGGI® Magic Sarap®. Heat 1 tbsp of oil in a nonstick pan and pour beaten eggs. Cook until barely set. Remove and set aside.
  3. Lightly sauté ham in 1 tbsp of oil. Remove and set aside.
  4. Heat 2 tbsp of oil in the same pan. Sauté chicken for 2mins per side. Remove and set aside
  5. Lightly spread butter on white bread. Toast until light golden brown.
  6. Slice chicken breast. Spread mayonnaise on toasted bread, assemble cheese, lettuce, tomato, ham, egg and chicken to make Clubhouse sandwich. Serve immediately.
