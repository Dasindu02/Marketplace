Healthy Snack Ideas for Weight Loss

Looking for ways to curb hunger between meals?
Apple Slices with Almond Butter

Apples are a great source of fiber, which helps keep you full and satisfied. Pairing them with
almond butter adds healthy fats and protein, making this snack more filling. The combination of
sweet and creamy textures also satisfies cravings for something indulgent.


Greek Yogurt with Berries

Greek yogurt is rich in protein, which is essential for muscle maintenance and can help you feel full longer.
Adding berries provides natural sweetness and a boost of antioxidants, which are beneficial for overall health.
This snack is also low in calories, making it a great choice for weight loss.


Veggie Sticks with Hummus

Fresh vegetables like carrots, cucumbers, and bell peppers are low in calories but high in vitamins, minerals,
and fiber. Hummus, made from chickpeas, adds a dose of protein and healthy fats. This combination not only keeps
you full but also satisfies your need for something crunchy and savory.


Hard-Boiled Eggs

Hard-boiled eggs are a portable, high-protein snack. Protein helps control hunger by slowing down digestion,
which keeps you feeling fuller for longer. Eggs are also rich in essential nutrients like vitamin D and B vitamins,
making them a nutritious choice.


Nuts and Seeds

A small handful of nuts or seeds provides a good balance of healthy fats, protein, and fiber. These nutrients work
together to keep hunger at bay. While nuts and seeds are calorie-dense, they are also nutrient-dense, so a small
portion can be very satisfying without leading to overeating.
