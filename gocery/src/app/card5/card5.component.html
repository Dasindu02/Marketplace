Quick and Nutritious Breakfast Ideas

Wondering what to have for dinner after a busy day at work?
Overnight Oats

Mix rolled oats with your choice of milk (dairy or plant-based), add a spoonful of yogurt,
some chia seeds, and sweeten with honey or maple syrup. Top with fresh fruits, nuts, or seeds.
Refrigerate overnight and enjoy in the morning.


Avocado Toast

Spread mashed avocado on whole-grain toast. Sprinkle with salt, pepper, and a squeeze of lemon juice.
Add toppings like a poached egg, cherry tomatoes, or smoked salmon for extra protein


Greek Yogurt with Berries

Take a bowl of Greek yogurt and top it with a handful of fresh berries, a drizzle of honey, and some granola
or nuts. This breakfast is high in protein and antioxidants.


Smoothie Bowl

Blend your favorite fruits (like bananas, berries, and mango) with some yogurt or a splash of milk. Pour into a
bowl and top with granola, chia seeds, coconut flakes, and a few more pieces of fruit.


Peanut Butter Banana Sandwich

Spread peanut butter on whole-grain bread and top with banana slices. Add a sprinkle of cinnamon for extra flavor.
You can also toast the sandwich for a warm option.


Omelette Wrap

Make a quick omelette with your favorite fillings (cheese, spinach, mushrooms, etc.), then wrap it in a whole-grain tortilla.
You can eat this on the go if you're in a hurry.
