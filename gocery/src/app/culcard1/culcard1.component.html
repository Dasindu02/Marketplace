Pineapple Chicken Salad


Prep Time: 30 mins | Cook Time: 45 mins | Servings: 4

★★★★☆

Ingredients

  • Pineapple (peeled and sliced) 400g
  • Chicken (boneless and roasted) 400g
  • Sweet Chille Sauce (3 tbsp)
  • Pineapple Flower ( Nicely wash and cleaned )
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Arrange the slices of Pineapple on either side of a long dish
  2. ⁠Mix the Roasted Chicken with 2 table spoons of Sweet Chillie Sauce and place it in the middle of the dish
  3. ⁠Pour the remaining Sauce over the Pineapple
  4. ⁠Garnish with the cleaned Pineapple Flower
